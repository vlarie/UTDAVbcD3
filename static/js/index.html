Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Socioeconomic Factors

Exploration of the interactive chart above reveals interesting relationships between various health risks such as obesity, smoking, and possession of healthcare coverage as they relate to poverty, age, and household income.

Data is drawn from 2014 ACS 1-year estimates collected from U.S. Census Bureau and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System and represented as medians and percentages for individual states in the US.

Mouseover circles to show details for each state.
